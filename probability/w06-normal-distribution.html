      ii. We need to find the equivalent d value. The standard deviation for D is $\\sqrt{16}=4$. A z-score of -4 corresponds to 4 standard deviations below the mean. So, $d = \\mu + z\\sigma = 35 + (-4)(4) = 35 - 16 = 19$.
